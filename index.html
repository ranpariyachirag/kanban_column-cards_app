Kanban Column-Cards App

Notes :

  • Click on "Add Column" button to add new column.
  • Click on "Add Card" button to add new card in perticular column.
  • Click on any Card to see or modify discription of that card and change the name of that card as well you will be able to add comments on that card.
  • Click on X symbol of any card to delete that card from column.
  • Click on "Delete Column" button to delete that column.
